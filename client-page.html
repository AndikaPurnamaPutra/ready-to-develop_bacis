CLIENT CHALLENGES

Businesses of all sizes are in need of high-quality, agile, done for or with you creative services.

But often clients don’t have the time (or experience) to source and work directly with creatives. This can lead to scope creep, project blockers, miscommunication, relationship breakdowns and incomplete or inadequate work.

FREELANCE CHALLENGES

Meanwhile, it can be difficult for creatives at every skill-level to juggle business admin, biz dev, and marketing with the actual work they sell to clients. These small business tasks can drain their imagination and focus, resulting in a loss for both creative and client.

project harmony

For nearly a decade, Indie-Life has successfully served businesses/ organizations of all sizes and sectors by fostering relationships with early stage and seasoned freelancers and going beyond big box talent warehouses to handle the business behind creativity. Our unique approach allows us to deliver superior service to clients and an equitable experience for creatives.
